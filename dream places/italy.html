Grand Canal (Venice)

The Grand Canal (“Canalasso”) is the main waterway in Venice. It is the largest canal and stretches about 3 km (2 miles) through the main island, forming a giant “S” curve, from the Santa Lucia train station to the Piazza San Marco and the stunning church of Santa Maria de Salute.
